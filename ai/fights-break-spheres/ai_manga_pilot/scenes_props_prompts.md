## Effect Language

- Humiliation: tight framing, tilted camera, crowd shadows leaning inward.
- Support: warm rim light on Xun'er, softened crowd blur behind.
- Ring clue: one-frame-like pulse, black-blue glow, no large explosion.
- Yunlan pressure: cold white robes, silver highlights, symmetrical hall, characters separated by empty floor space.
