# Episode 01 Script: 《斗之力，三段》

## Metadata

- Format: AI manga drama storyboard package.
- Layout: 9:16 vertical.
- Runtime target: 90 seconds.
- Visual style: young shonen manga, high-contrast light, sharp linework, expressive faces.
- Source scope: chapters 1-4, adapted and compressed.
- Ending hook: Nalan Yanran is not here for a friendly visit.

## Logline

Once hailed as the brightest youth of the Xiao clan, Xiao Yan is publicly branded a failure. One quiet supporter, one strange black ring, and one cold guest from the Yunlan Sect turn his humiliation into the first spark of a larger storm.

## Beat Script

### 00:00-00:20 Public Test

Voiceover: Wutan City, Xiao clan training square. One stone tablet can decide a young person's worth.

Tester: "Xiao Yan. Dou strength, third stage. Low rank."

The crowd laughs. Xiao Yan keeps his face still, but his fist tightens until his nails press into his palm.

Crowd whispers:

- "Wasn't he the clan's miracle?"
- "Three years, and still falling."
- "Without his father, he would have been sent away."

Xiao Mei steps up next. The tablet shines. The crowd cheers for her talent.

Then Xiao Xun'er takes the test. The light rises higher, and the square falls silent.

Tester: "Xiao Xun'er. Ninth stage. High rank."

### 00:20-00:32 Xun'er's Support

Xun'er walks past the admiring crowd and stops in front of Xiao Yan.

Xun'er: "Brother Xiao Yan, lift your head."

Xiao Yan: "Do I still deserve that name?"

Xun'er: "The person who taught me to let go and stand again should not forget his own words."

Xiao Yan cannot answer. The cheers behind him feel further away than ever.

### 00:32-00:52 Night Cliff

Voiceover: This is Dou Qi Continent, where strength writes the rules and weakness is remembered louder than kindness.

Under the moon, Xiao Yan lies alone on a cliff. He remembers a secret no one else knows: his soul came from another world.

Xiao Yan: "If I was brought here just to become a joke, then this world has a cruel sense of humor."

He touches the old black ring on his finger, the last keepsake from his mother.

The ring gives off a faint glimmer, then goes dark.

### 00:52-01:04 Father's Deadline

Xiao Zhan appears from the forest, trying to sound casual.

Xiao Zhan: "Still thinking about the test?"

Xiao Yan: "It was expected."

Xiao Zhan tells him the family rule: one year remains. If Xiao Yan cannot recover enough strength, even the clan chief's son cannot keep his place.

Xiao Yan smiles for his father.

Xiao Yan: "Then I will reach it."

But after Xiao Zhan leaves, the smile fades.

### 01:04-01:22 Guest Hall

Morning. Xiao Yan enters the clan guest hall and finds no seat prepared for him.

The elders pretend it is a mistake. The young disciples laugh quietly.

Xun'er's voice cuts through the room.

Xun'er: "Brother Xiao Yan, sit here."

The laughter stops. Xiao Yan sits beside her and notices the strangers in moon-white robes.

One old man is powerful. One young man watches the girl beside him. The girl looks calm, cold, and certain of her place.

### 01:22-01:30 Yunlan Hook

Xun'er notices Xiao Yan staring at the silver cloud-and-sword mark on their sleeves.

Xun'er: "Yunlan Sect."

Xiao Yan stiffens.

Xun'er: "And that girl is Nalan Yanran."

Cut to the girl's calm eyes.

Voiceover: Some guests bring gifts. Some bring storms.

End card: Next episode: The Broken Engagement.
