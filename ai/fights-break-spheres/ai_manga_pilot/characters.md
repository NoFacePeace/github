# Character Design Bible

Use these tags consistently in every image prompt. Keep all teen characters modestly styled and age-appropriate.

## Global Character Style

- Young shonen manga design, clean ink lines, expressive eyes, strong silhouettes.
- Xuanhuan-inspired Chinese fantasy clothing, no modern logos.
- Avoid photorealism, sexualized posing, revealing outfits, excessive gore, or unreadable generated text.
- Use vertical 9:16 composition with clear face visibility and space for subtitles.

## Xiao Yan

- Tag: `XY_black_training_robes`
- Role: Fallen former prodigy, fifteen-year-old Xiao clan disciple.
- Look: Lean teenage boy, short messy black hair, sharp dark eyes, simple black training robe with subtle clan trim, old black ring on right hand.
- Expression range: silent pain, restrained anger, stubborn smile, lonely focus.
- Gesture motifs: clenched fist, touching the black ring, looking away from crowds, standing despite pressure.
